# Governance

cloud-itonami-isic-5630 follows open-source governance with transparent
decision-making documented in ADRs.

Scope: Back-office service-record logging, staffing scheduling, supply-order
coordination, guest-safety-concern escalation.
Out-of-scope: Any responsible-service-of-alcohol (RSA) authority decision
(continuing/authorizing service to an intoxicated patron, overriding an
age-verification/ID-check failure, serving a minor), and any direct
equipment control (taps, POS terminals, access control).

See README.md for complete CRITICAL scope exclusions.
